<h4><strong>Volleyball</strong></h4>
<p>Volleyball is a net barrier team-sport game. Students practice the game
through drills that involves serving, setting, throwing, passing, digging
and spiking. Techniques and strategies of attacking and defending are also
taught through activities and practices. This helps to hone students’ fundamental
skills, while at the same time, develops students’ fitness conditioning.</p>
<p>Being a team-sport, volleyball gives students the opportunity to demonstrate
sportsmanship during training and competitions. Volleyball also opens up
platforms for students to demonstrate their leadership capabilities to
lead and influence members with positive attitudes and qualities.</p>
